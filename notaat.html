NOTAAT = Not One Thing At A time

NOTAAT is a collective by Sultana and Puneet.
Under the framework of this collective- we are not one thing, we are many things. We are not artists or scientists or humans. We are slime mould and mycelium and hands and we are rene magraite. We do not produce work. Our medium is conversation and our work as a collective is to uphold this collective.
